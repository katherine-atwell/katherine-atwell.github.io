About Me

My name is Katherine Atwell and I am a prospective dual degree student graduating in May 2020 with a B.S. in Computer Science and a B.S. in Mathematics. I'm currently looking for entry-level positions, and am particularly interested in positions that would allow me to use both my mathematical and my computational skills. I am proficient in C++, Python, C, HTML, CSS, and Javascript and also have experience coding in Assembly and VHDL. Between my internships and classes, I have experience with UI design and frontend, backend, and full-stack programming. However, I am most interested in a position that is heavily backend, with a particular interest in AI/ML.

In terms of work experience, I worked as an research intern for Iowa State University in the summer of 2017 and interned for Google in the summers of 2018 and 2019. My first summer, I worked closely with two other interns to design the UI and UX for a piece of software that simplifies the number of steps required for Traffic Incident Managers in Iowa. The paper that we helped co-write was published in the conference proceedings of the HFES 2018 conference.
